1 . Management consulting involves advising organizations on various aspects of their operations to improve efficiency, effectiveness, and overall performance. Management consultants work with businesses of all sizes, across different industries, and offer strategic guidance to tackle challenges and capitalize on opportunities. They may be hired for short-term projects or long-term engagements, depending on the client's needs.

  1. Strategy Development: Helping businesses create long-term plans and identifying opportunities for growth and competitive advantage.
